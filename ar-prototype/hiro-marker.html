📷 Hiro AR Marker

Print this page or display it on another screen, then point the WebAR camera at it.

How to use:
  1. Print this marker or open it on a second screen (phone/tablet).
  2. Go back to the WebAR app and choose Marker Mode.
  3. Point your MacBook camera at the printed/displayed marker.
  4. 3D objects will appear anchored on top of the marker!
  5. Use the toolbar to change object shape and color.
